The Problem
"Every engagement starts from scratch. Hours of Excel work before any real analysis begins."

Government procurement consultants and agency CPOs are sitting on goldmines of spend data — but the tools to analyze it don't exist at the right level. Enterprise platforms like Coupa and Jaggaer cost millions and take months to deploy. General BI tools have no concept of NIGP taxonomy. So everyone does it manually.

The result: the most important procurement intelligence — the kind that could save agencies millions and protect taxpayers — stays buried in spreadsheets.

  • ⏱
    Hours lost to classificationBefore a consultant can analyze spend, they spend half a day mapping columns and classifying transactions against NIGP codes manually.
  • 📊
    No NIGP-native tool existsEvery tool in the market speaks UNSPSC or custom taxonomies. Nothing is built natively around the 18th Edition NIGP code that governs 33 states and thousands of local agencies.
  • 🔍
    Risk buried in plain sightMaverick spend, PO splitting, single-source concentration — these compliance risks are present in almost every agency's data. But finding them manually takes days.
  • 💸
    Enterprise tools aren't built for governmentThe platforms used by Fortune 500 procurement teams are inaccessible to most government agencies — too expensive, too complex, and not NIGP-aware.
